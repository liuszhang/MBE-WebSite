We have created an interactive video to help educate and illustrate what model based enterprise is and how it is revolutionizing the supply chain management and manufacturing. Through this new technology, suppliers will benefit through the reduction of error rate and re-work as well as reduced labor in data and document creation. MBE also reduces the time to mission by making it possible to quickly produce ‘build-to’ packages, that is, sets of documents that contain all data required to manufacture components, and provide all the information you need to begin production.
